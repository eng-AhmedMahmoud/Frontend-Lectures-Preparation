Grandma's Cookie Recipe

Kitchen Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Wooden spoon
  • Baking tray

Baking Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180 °C (356 °F).
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Fold in flour and chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto tray and bake for 12 minutes.
